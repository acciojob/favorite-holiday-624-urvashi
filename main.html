Holiday Destination - Shimla


The capital city of Himachal Pradesh, Shimla is one of the top tourist places in India. A gorgeous hill station with a rich colonial past, an iconic Mall road and a plethora of adventurous activities, it is immensely popular among honeymoon couples.
